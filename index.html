FAQ

Is the entire aquarium open?+
Yes — every exhibit and gallery is open. Shark Tunnel, Ocean Realm, Hippo Haven, all of it. Bring your wine.
How many wineries are there?+
40+ wineries pouring unlimited tastings all night — wines from around the world. Reds, whites, rosés, sparkling — something for everyone.
Is food available?+
Yes — cheese boards, charcuterie, and small plates from local vendors are available for purchase throughout the event.
Is there parking?+
On-site parking at the aquarium is $15. Rideshare drop-off is right out front and highly recommended.
What if it rains?+
The entire event is indoors. Rain, shine, or snow — we're pouring.
Can I touch the animals?+
Touch tanks with stingrays, sharks, and horseshoe crabs are open during select hours with supervision.
Will this sell out?+
It sold out last year. If you're reading this and tickets are still available, that's your sign.
